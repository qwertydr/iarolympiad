About IROLYM

The International Research Olympiad is dedicated to fostering academic excellence through global research competitions and collaborations.

Our Mission

To create a global platform that recognizes and nurtures exceptional research talent across all academic disciplines, promoting innovation, critical thinking, and international collaboration.

Since our founding in 2010, we have organized over 50 international competitions with participants from 120 countries, helping young researchers gain recognition and advance their careers.

Our Competitions

We organize world-class research competitions across multiple disciplines and academic levels.
